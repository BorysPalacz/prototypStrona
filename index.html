Designing parts and machines


I design parts and machines for various types of industries. I use modern software that allows me to create construction documentation. Most often I carry out projects concerning:

  • Creating documentation for existing parts.
  • Preparing of 3D models based on the provided 2D documentation.
  • Redesigning parts and machines
  • Designing devices and workshop parts
  • Modification and adaptation of older machines to quality requirements.


    • Technological support
